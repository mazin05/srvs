Curriculum

For Early Years Programme

Nursery:

In SRVS we follow both Montessori and kindergarten methods of teaching through integrated curriculum, as these are the 'Foundation years' during which early linguistic and arithmetic basics, scope for creative play and expression, physical fitness and constructive socialization are stressed upon to build childhood competencies.

For Grades I to V

The Primary School Programme:

The Primary school comprises of Grade I to V. The Teaching Pedagogy of these years focuses on developing the intellectual capabilities. Students of primary school are encouraged to participate is a lot of extra-curricular activities.

For Grades VI to VIII

The Middle School Programme:

At Grade VI to VIII, the programme is designed as a Pre-CBSE foundation level programme in which several a the skills stressed in the CBSE are developed early. The academic programme promotes breadth of study, that is invaluable to students as they prepare for Grade IX and X.

For Grades IX and X

The Senior School Programme:

As students progress through the senior years, we expect them to become increasingly independant learners with an interest in setting personal academic goals. The High School also provides training to enhance communication skills and encourages students to participate is a wide range of inter-house and inter-school competitions in cultural and sporting activities.

Assessment & Evaluation tests

Weekly Tests: Every week there is a test in each subject to check how the lessons are going down in the class as will as how seriously the children are applying themselves to grasp and learn from what is being taught. The determinant factors in the weekly assessment are industry, achievement and efficiency.

Exams: Along with weekly test, the school has two terminal exams and three mid-terminal exams in a year.
