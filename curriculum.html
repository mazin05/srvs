Curriculum

For Classes I to X

Religious Instruction, English, Mathematics, Second Languages Tamil, and French, General Science, Social Science, Computer Science, Drawing & Needle Work, Physical Education and Music.

For Higher Secondary

1 Part I Tamil/French
2 Part II English
3 Part II - Group I Physics, Chemistry, Maths and Computer Science
4 Group II Physics, Chemistry, Maths and Biology
5 Group II Business Mathematics/Computer Science, Commerce, Accountancy and Economics

Assessment & Evaluation tests

Weekly Tests: Every week there is a test in each subject to check how the lessons are going down in the class as will as how seriously the children are applying themselves to grasp and learn from what is being taught. The determinant factors in the weekly assessment are industry, achievement and efficiency.

Exams: Along with weekly test, the school has two terminal exams and three mid-terminal exams in a year.
