Facilities

Smart Class Rooms

Digital Smart Board enhances learning experience not only for students but also for teachers. It provides students with an enriched learning experience by encouraging the use of multimedia- rich elements like images, visuals, videos, simulations, presentations, etc.
It makes differentiated learning easier as teachers can cater to different learning needs of every student. In short, learning comes to life when infused with visual elements.

Library with over 10,000+ Books.

The availability of library facilities in educational institutes is essential for enhancing learning and facilitating intellectual growth for students. At SRVS, we provide access to diverse resources, including books, journals, magazines, newspapers, and digital resources for our students. We believe library facilities serve valuable resource and promotes lifelong learning among students. We provide opportunities for personal growth, self-study and encourage continuous learning in our students.
